Adding Dictionary and Language Pack

You can add dictionaries  and language packs to spellchecker quickly and easily check spellings in Odia and other language correspondence​.

Dynamic Spell Checker

Dynamic Spelling will add the red squiggly line under any words that it believes are misspelt. It will add a green squiggly line under words where it believes there is a grammar problem.

Static Spell Checker

Static Spell Checker remove extra space and Paragraph lines to find appropriate word from database. Also finding word and replacing it with an alternative word.
