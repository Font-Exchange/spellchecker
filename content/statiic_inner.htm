Static spell checker

 

Treat

  1. Remove extra space.
  2. Paragraph lines to find appropriate word from database.
  3. finding word and replacing it with an alternative word.

Auto Correction

  1. Auto correction, also known as text replacement.  If you type misspell word, the spell Checker  replace correct word.
  2. Right check mark is default [Auto correction mode enable]
  3. If the check mark is off Auto correction was not function properly.

Spell Check

  1. Checking text matter and suggest correct word from database.

Misspell word

  1. After pressing Spell Check Button the misspell or unknown word is highlight [Red color]. mouse over the misspell word and click correct word in suggestion box below.

 

Option Box [Spellcheck area]

  1. First Word: Press control button while clicking the word if right side characters are correct. Left suggestions will be offered.
  2. Last Word: Press shift button while clicking the word if left side characters are correct. Right suggestions will be offered.

 

Know about color words

  1. Red : Misspell or unknown Word
  2. Black : Offensive Word

 

Offensive word detector

  1. Spell Checker automatically detect offensive word. There are three option (partially, exactly and complementary, the default value is partially)
  2. you can changed option value.
